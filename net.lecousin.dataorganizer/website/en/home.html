DataOrganizer is a free software that allows you organizing your videos and music in the best way (and other types of data in the future).

Main features:

  • Arrange your data by type, with a hierarchy of labels
  • Search your data thanks to the advanced search:
    • By name, rating, date added, last time opened...
    • By type, by labels...
    • According to its type: by artist, by actor, by duration...
  • Rate your data, put comments on them
  • Fill information about your data, or simply retrieve them from Internet (automatically):
    • Video: poster, previews (automatically taken from the video), list of actors, directors, producers, writers, synopsis, comments and rating from internet users...
    • Audio: album covers, images, list of tracks with information, artist and album name, year, ...
  • Play your media:
    • Directly in DataOrganizer thanks to our integrated Media Player
    • By using an installed application
    • Play it directly on your TV (by using you Internet Box if it supports it, i.e. SFR Neuf Box Media Center)
  • And various other features
